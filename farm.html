About Firozpur

A historic city where tradition and progress blend beautifully

Firozpur (also spelled Ferozepur) is a historic city in Punjab, India, located near the Indo-Pak border. Known for its military legacy, cultural richness, and peaceful rural life, it's a place where tradition and progress blend beautifully.

  • Rich History

    Founded in the 14th century, Firozpur has witnessed centuries of history and cultural evolution.

  • Military Heritage

    Hosts one of India's oldest army cantonments with a proud military tradition.

  • Cultural Sites

    Rich in Gurudwaras, Temples & Historical Sites that reflect Punjab's diverse heritage.

  • Agricultural Heartland

    Surrounded by lush agricultural land and traditional farming communities.

About Virk Farm House

Our family heritage in the heart of Punjab

Our Family Legacy

Virk Farm House is our family farmhouse located just outside the city of Firozpur. It reflects our connection to Punjab's agricultural roots, rural tranquility, and community values.

The farm offers open green fields, peaceful views, and a space full of simplicity and warmth. It's a place where generations of our family have lived, worked, and celebrated life.

Experience Rural Punjab

At Virk Farm House, you can experience the authentic rural life of Punjab:

  • Stroll through our organic fields and orchards
  • Experience traditional Punjabi hospitality
  • Enjoy home-cooked meals with family recipes
  • Participate in seasonal farming activities
